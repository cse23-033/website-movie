ABOUT

Bring nearly 100 years of Movie and Television History to life like never before when you visit the Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood. Bolster your tour experience with this amazing app which instantly plays videos and showcases photos from your favorite films and television shows that were shot at the exact locations you are seeing! The app also lets you know the best vantage points to take photos during your tour (and you can even decorate your photos with stickers of WB favorites of the big and small screens!). Along with interactive quizzes, the Warner Bros. Studio Tour app enhances the experience of an attraction that has long been a must-see for visiting entertainment fans around the world.
